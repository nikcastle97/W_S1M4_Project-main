Who Is She..?

Taylor Swift has become a worldwide phenomena of unparralleded proportions! Not only has she succeeded in winning over the entirety of Pop-Music Culture's Fan-Base, but football fans in addition have been fawning over this celebrity sensation as of late with her courtship of Travis Kelsy.
The "Eras Tour" has become a phenomena of its own as well! With Fans filling theatres world-wide, her success and fame have spread to nearly every corner of the globe. She is truly a magnificent artist whom we all love and adore.

Her Albums To Date!

"Taylor Swift"

"Taylor Swift", being the title of her very first album may seem a bit on the nose, but it seems to have worked in her favor. With its initial release in late October of 2006, fans quickly rallied to the 11-track standard.

"Fearless"

In mid-to-early November, and in the midst of spooks and turkeys, Taylor released her second studio recorded album. "Fearless".

"Speak Now"

October 25th, 2010 greeted Taylor's third release, "Speak Now".

"Red"

Released as expected late October yet again in 2012, "Red" helped to push Taylor's sound in an entirely new direction. Mixing elements of pop-country, rock and dubstep in an entirely new way for the artist to make something phenominal!

"1989"

Instead of releasing "1989" outright on all platforms with the initial release on October 27th, 2014, Taylor decided to withhold on streaming and release only with hard-copies.

"reputation"

Delving into a better picture of the Taylor we know today, "reputation" excited some and offended a few with its real-to-a-fault attitude. This album was released on November 10th, 2017.

"Lover"

"Lover", released on August 23, 2019, was the first album of Taylor's to not only switch it up and release more than a month before any of its predecessors, but it was also noted for its queer-coated nuiances and themes which set fans a-blaze in support! This album in particular prooved inovative on a different level than previous ventures of the young singer/song-writer.

"Folklore"

Turning directions yet again, Taylor Swift released "Folklore", a more lo-fi version of her trademark style of sound. After its release on July 24th, 2020, it became a sensation only to be coupled with and followed by its successor...

"Evermore"

"Evermore" directly following its counterpart "Folklore" and released on December 11th at the fatal end of the dreaded year of 2020. It quickly climbed the song-charts like evry other Taylor Swift release to date; sensationally...

"Midnights"

And that leave us with "midnights", the current favorite album of many a' swifties to date. Its release on October 21st oof 2022 excited and intrigued fans after nearly two year without a new release.
